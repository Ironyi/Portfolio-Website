Visually Triggered Drum Sequencer

A four button keyboard and step sequencer with a thermal camera gimmick. It is all powered by a Teensy 4.1 microcontroller. The drum sequencer has 3 different channels: kick, snare, and hi-hat. Some yellow LEDs show which beat you are currently on, 1-4, and another set of green LEDs tell you which instrument you are on. The thermal camera is interactive, displaying heat signatures onto a color-coded 8x8 heatmap showing you how hot or cold the visual is.

Analog Synthesizer

An eight button analog button-controlled keyboard synthesizer made purely with an Operational Amplifier and Voltage.
